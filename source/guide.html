🍎 iPhone — install first, then enable notifications

⚠️ iPhone note: Push notifications only work when BirdieFriends is opened from the home screen icon — not from Safari. Skip this step and you won't get Birdie Alerts or CttP updates during the round.

🆕 New Events button

When new events are added to the calendar, a gold 🆕 N button appears in the header. Tap it to open your New Events queue — a focused view of new additions only. Register, park, or scroll past. Tap ✓ Done when finished.

Tip: Events you've registered for stay pinned to My Events permanently. Events you're not interested in can be swiped left to Parked to clear the clutter.

👈 How to park an event

Can't make it to a round? Swipe left on the event card. The card slides off and moves to your Parked tab. You'll see a brief Parked ✓ confirmation with an Undo option — tap it within 4 seconds if you swiped by mistake.

🅿️ The Parked tab

Parked events live in the Parked tab in the bottom nav. They're still fully actionable — you can register from there anytime, which automatically brings the event back to My Events.

Note: You can't park an event you're already registered for. Unregister first, then swipe to park.

🏌️ Register to play

Tap the green Register button on any event card. Your status changes to ✅ Going and the event stays pinned to My Events.

🔄 Register as Sub

If an event is full, you'll see Register as Sub. You're on the waitlist. If a spot opens, watch for a push notification — you'll be promoted automatically.

❌ Unregister

Plans changed? Tap Unregister on the event card or the "Can't Make It" button in your Schedule tab. Please unregister early so others can take your spot.

👥 See who's playing

Tap Players › on any event card to see the full registered player list, including anyone on sub status.

⏳ The 5th Player — Read This

BirdieFriends events are organized in groups of 4. If you're the 5th player to register for an 8-player event, your spot is tentatively held — not yet confirmed.

Your spot locks in automatically when a 6th player registers, completing the second group. Until then, your event card shows a gold ⏳ Spot not yet confirmed warning.

⚠️ Always check the player count on your event card before heading to the course. If you're still showing as the 5th player, contact the Commissioner.

Tip: BF Series events don't have this limitation — they use a flexible grouping system. The 5th player rule applies only to standard 4-player group events.

🗓️ What you're committed to

The Schedule tab shows only events you've registered for, in date order. Each row shows your status (✅ Going or 🔄 Sub) and a full-width Unregister button.

Tip: Check your Schedule the night before any weekend — it's the quickest way to confirm everything you've committed to.

🦅 Birdie Alert

Made a birdie? Tap Birdie Alert, select your name, tap the hole number, and tap Announce. A push notification fires to all BirdieFriends — the community celebrates with you. If someone already birdied that hole, the app announces the skin is busted.

🎯 Closest to the Pin (CttP)

On designated holes (#3, #8, #10, #15, #18), tap the hole pill, enter your distance in feet (optional), and tap Closest on #X. The live leaderboard updates immediately and a push goes out announcing the new leader.

Last valid submission per hole wins. Honor system — submit your own distance, not a teammate's. The leaderboard refreshes every 60 seconds.

Note: Uncontested CttP holes (only one entry, or no entries) roll into skins. CttP and skins are independent of each other.

Step 7
End Round — Scorecards

🏁 Who enters scorecards?

Each group's overseer (designated by the Commissioner before the round) enters scores after play is complete. Tap End Round — Enter Scorecards in the live panel to open the scorecard entry form.

📋 How to enter scores

For each player, select their result on each hole using the dropdown. You're entering Stableford points — not stroke scores. Use the chart below:

Result Points Notes
🦅 Birdie 4 The goal!
🏌️ Par 2 Solid result
Bogey 1 1 over par
Bogey+ (Double or worse) 0 No points
🦅🦅 Eagle 6 2 under par
🦅🦅🦅 Albatross 8 3 under par
Note: Enter points — not strokes. A 4 on a par-3 is a Bogey (1 point), not entered as "4". When in doubt, ask the Commissioner.

Step 8
Results

🏆 After the round

Tap Results in the bottom nav for links to:

  • 📊 Series Standings — your flight ranking and series totals
  • 📋 Event Results — individual event scorecards
  • 👤 My Game — your personal history across all events
  • 👥 Groupings — your tee time and playing partners (published before each event)

👥 Finding your Groupings and Tee Time

The Commissioner publishes groupings before each event. Tap Results → Groupings to see your tee time, playing partners, and cart assignments. Check this the night before or morning of the event.

📊 Series Standings

The BF Series tracks points across all Series events in three flights: Green, Combo, and Gold. Standings update after each event is scored. Find your ranking in Results → Series Standings.

Notifications

🔔 What you'll receive

  • 🦅 Birdie alerts during live events
  • 🎯 New CttP leader announced on each hole

🔕 Toggle notifications

Tap the bell icon (🔔 or 🔕) in the header anytime. You can also manage notifications in About (tap the ℹ️ icon). When you enable notifications, a push subscription is stored under your name so the Commissioner can reach you directly.

iPhone reminder: You must open BirdieFriends from the home screen icon (installed as PWA) to receive push notifications. The Safari browser tab will not receive them.

Troubleshooting
❓ I don't see the live banner during the event
The live banner only appears for registered players. Check that you're registered for the event (your card should show ✅ Going) and that you've selected your name (initials pill top-right). If you're registered and still don't see it, close and reopen the app.
❓ I'm not getting push notifications
iPhone: Make sure you opened the app from your home screen icon, not Safari. Then tap the 🔕 bell and allow notifications. Android: Tap the bell, allow notifications if prompted. If the bell shows 🔔 but you still get nothing, tap it twice to reset your subscription.
❓ I can't find my name in the player list
You need to be a registered BirdieFriends member to appear in the list. Contact the Commissioner to get added — it only takes a minute.
❓ My event disappeared from My Events
You may have accidentally swiped it to Parked. Tap the 🅿️ Parked tab in the bottom nav and check there — you can register or unpark it from there.
❓ Register button isn't showing
If the event is within 48 hours and has more than 4 players registered, registration locks to allow group management. Contact the Commissioner directly to be added.
❓ Something else isn't working
Text the Commissioner: 617-710-4755. For a quick fix, try pulling down to refresh or closing and reopening the app from the home screen icon.
