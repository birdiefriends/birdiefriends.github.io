BirdieFriends brings together everything you need for competitive recreational golf — from weekly tee time reservations to season-long championship tracking.

Weekly Event Registration
Reserve your spot for upcoming events in seconds. View the schedule, confirm attendance, and always know when and where the next round is happening.

Championship Series
Compete across 8 events in the annual BF Championship. Rolling standings, handicap-adjusted quotas, and prize payouts track who's playing their best golf all season.

Live Scorecard Entry
Submit your hole-by-hole points right from the course. Scores flow automatically into the results system — no paperwork, no manual entry delays.
