Step 1
Getting Started
Open the portal Go to birdiefriends.com/portal.html in your browser, or tap the BirdieFriends icon on your home screen if you've installed it.
Select your name Tap the player pill in the top right corner — it shows BH initials or "Guest." Pick your name from the list. This is how the app knows who you are.
Install to your home screen For the best experience — and to receive push notifications — add BirdieFriends to your home screen.
Enable notifications Tap the 🔕 bell icon in the header and allow notifications. You'll get live birdie alerts, CttP updates, and event news.

🍎 iPhone users — install first, then enable notifications

  1. Open birdiefriends.com/portal.html in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button ⎋ at the bottom
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Open BirdieFriends from your home screen icon
  5. Select your name, then tap the 🔕 bell to enable notifications

⛳ Your personal event feed

My Events shows all upcoming BirdieFriends events by default. New events appear here automatically — no action needed to see them.

Events you've registered for stay here permanently. Events you're not interested in can be swiped away to Parked.

🆕 New Events button

When new events are added to the calendar, a gold 🆕 N button appears in the header. Tap it to open your New Events queue — a focused view showing only the new additions. Register, swipe to park, or scroll past. Tap ✓ Done when finished.

Tip: The count in the section label (e.g. "Upcoming (4)") shows how many events are on your My Events screen. Tap "N parked →" to jump to events you've set aside.

👈 How to park an event

Can't make it to a round? Swipe left on the event card on My Events. The card slides off and moves to your Parked tab.

You'll see a brief Parked confirmation with an Undo option — tap it within 4 seconds if you swiped by mistake.

🅿️ The Parked tab

Parked events live in the Parked tab in the bottom nav. They're still fully actionable — you can register from there anytime, which brings the event back to My Events automatically.

Note: You can't park an event you're registered for. Cancel your registration first, then swipe to park it.
✅
Step 4
Registering for Events

🏌️ Register to play

Tap the green Register button on any event card. Your spot is confirmed immediately and the event stays pinned to My Events.

🔄 Register as Sub

If an event is full, you'll see Register as Sub. You're on the waitlist — if a spot opens, you may be promoted. Watch for a push notification if that happens.

❌ Unregister

Plans changed? Tap Unregister on the event card. The event will stay on My Events until you park it. Please unregister early so others can take your spot.

👥 See who's playing

Tap Players › on any event card to see the full registered player list for that event.

🗓️
Step 5
Your Schedule

🗓️ What you're committed to

The Schedule tab shows only events you've registered for, displayed as a clean date list with your status (✅ Going or 🔄 Sub).

Tap any row to jump directly to that event card on My Events — where you can unregister, see the player list, or get directions.

Tip: Check Schedule before the weekend to see everything you've signed up for at a glance.
⚡
Step 6
During a Live Round

⚡ Live Event Banner

When a BF Series event tee time arrives, a gold banner appears at the top of My Events for registered players. Tap it to expand the live panel.

🦅 Birdie Alert 🎯 Closest to Pin 🏁 End Round

🦅 Birdie Alert

Made a birdie? Tap Birdie Alert, select your name, tap the hole number, and tap Announce. A push notification fires to all BirdieFriends — the community celebrates with you.

Skin detection is automatic — if someone already birdied that hole, the app announces the skin is busted.

🎯 Closest to the Pin (CttP)

On designated holes (#3, #8, #10, #15, #18), tap the hole pill, enter your distance (optional, in feet), and tap Closest on #X. The live leaderboard updates immediately and a push goes out.

Last submission per hole wins — honor system. Leaderboard refreshes every 60 seconds.

🏁 End Round — Enter Scorecards

After the round, overseers tap End Round — Enter Scorecards to enter each player's point scores hole by hole.

Point values: Albatross 8 · Eagle 6 · Birdie 4 · Par 2 · Bogey 1 · Bogey+ 0

Note: The live banner only appears for registered players. Non-registered players won't see it.
🏆
Step 7
Results

🏆 After the round

Tap Results in the bottom nav to find links to event results, series standings, your personal game history (My Game), and groupings for upcoming events.

📊 Series Standings

The BF Series tracks points across all Series events. Standings update after each event is scored. Check your flight ranking and series total in Results → Series Standings.

🔔
Step 8
Notifications

🔔 What you'll receive

When notifications are enabled, you'll get push alerts for:

  • 🦅 Birdie alerts during live events
  • 🎯 New CttP leader on each hole
  • 📣 Messages from the Commissioner
  • 🎉 Sub promotion (when a spot opens for you)

🔕 Toggle notifications

Tap the bell icon (🔔 or 🔕) in the header anytime to turn notifications on or off. You can also manage this in About (tap the ⓘ icon in the header).

iPhone reminder: Notifications only work when BirdieFriends is installed as a PWA (added to home screen). Opening in Safari browser won't receive push notifications.
